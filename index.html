Barbara Howard

International News Anchor

Peabody Award-winning anchor, reporter, writer, editor, producer with extensive local, national and international experience. Work chosen for "Best of NPR". Four years anchoring WGBH's "All Things Considered" (2015 - 2019). In 2018, awarded New York Festivals bronze trophy for "Best News Feature". Formerly longtime fill-in anchor and editor at WBUR. International reporting while living three years in Berlin and then three years in Beijing, producing sound-rich features for NPR, BBC, CBC, VOA and Marketplace. Associate Producer and Audio Producer for the PBS Civil Rights series “Eyes on the Prize". Winner of WBUR's first Peabody Award as Executive Producer for conceptualizing, reporting, writing and voicing the four-part series “Liberation Remembered”.
